• Let's move on to our activities. What is this Alpha Shares? Entrepreneurs with projects come to the Alpha Community and apply. After their applications are reviewed and they receive approval for investment, they start selling their projects. They explain what percentage of the profits they will be able to give as a share. For example: Project X guarantees a 10% share of the profits. They expect a $10,000 donation from the Alpha community for this 10% profit share guarantee. These donations are transferred to the wallets of entrepreneurs through Alpha. After starting to earn income from the project, the entrepreneur who received the investment sends 10% of the profits to the community pool. This profit share is distributed among the Alpha holders. This way, a secure bridge is established between Alpha investors and entrepreneurs. With this system, more entrepreneurs can be supported, and the reputation and usage of Alpha can be expanded.

• Another event we have is Earn-Burn: To participate in the event, everyone must send 1000 Alpha to the organizer's empty wallet. (As the value of Alpha increases, the minimum required Alpha value will decrease.) All wallets sending Alpha are recorded, and their names are written on a wheel. Then the wheel is spun, and the lucky winner is sent half of the collected Alpha. The remaining Alpha is sent to a burn wallet, reducing the total supply.
