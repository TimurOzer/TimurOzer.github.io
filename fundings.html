AlphaRooms

AlphaRooms is inspired by the Backrooms lore and aims to incorporate infinite levels into our game with the help of Alpha Community. The game is designed to be an infinitely solo-based experience, with the main focus being exploration of infinite levels. AlphaRooms isn't just a game; it's also a crypto project driven by the Alpha Community. Following the early access release, we plan to update weekly to add new levels, features, and entities to enhance your experience. Additionally, 50% of the revenue generated from the sale of this game will be used to purchase our own coin, Alpha.
